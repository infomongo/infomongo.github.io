My background is in interaction design. This is the practice of making software easy to use. It starts by understanding the people using it, who they are what they want. A well designed tool approaches the problem from the user’s perspective. My approach to writing is similar.

The Game Plan

  1. Learn about your audience. Who they are and what they want.
  2. Understand the tone you want to project.
  3. Identify your goal. Are we educating or persuading? What does a win look like?
  4. Write the piece. Edit it. Get feedback from you. Revise it. Repeat until you are happy.
  5. Promote the post on social media and my blog. (Unless I ghostwrite it for you.)
  6. Send you an invoice, which I hope you’ll pay promptly.
